About PesoCheck

PesoCheck is an innovative web application designed to combat counterfeit currency in the Philippines by helping users verify the authenticity of Philippine banknotes. Using advanced machine learning, PesoCheck distinguishes between genuine and fake money, empowering individuals and businesses to protect themselves from financial fraud.

Our Mission

In the Philippines, where cash transactions drive daily commerce, counterfeit banknotes undermine economic trust. PesoCheck delivers an accessible, portable tool to detect fake currency, empowering Filipinos to verify security features and safeguard financial stability.

Features

  • Counterfeit Detection: Upload an image of a Philippine banknote, and PesoCheck will analyze it to determine whether it is genuine or counterfeit, supporting both paper and polymer notes.
  • History Log: View a detailed history of past analysis results, including the image, classification (real, fake, or invalid), confidence score, and timestamp, for easy reference and tracking.
  • Invalid Image Detection: PesoCheck identifies non-banknote images (e.g., random objects) and flags them as invalid, ensuring accurate results for Philippine currency only.

Technology

PesoCheck is powered by a ResNet18 convolutional neural network, a model pre-trained on ImageNet and fine-tuned for our specific use case. The model was trained over 100 epochs on a carefully curated dataset, consisting of:

  • 410 images of genuine New Generation Currency paper Philippine banknotes.
  • 410 images of play money, representing counterfeit banknotes.
  • 450 images of random objects, used to train the system to recognize invalid inputs.
